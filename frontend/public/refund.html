Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. After cancelling, you'll keep Pro access until the end of your current paid period, and you won't be charged again. To cancel, use the link in your purchase receipt email (from Paddle) or contact us and we'll help.

Auto-renewal

Pro subscriptions renew automatically (monthly or yearly) until you cancel.

Refunds

  • If you're not happy, you can request a refund within 7 days of your first payment — email us and we'll arrange it.
  • For renewals, we generally don't offer refunds for partial periods already started, but reach out and we'll consider reasonable requests fairly.
  • Refunds are processed through Paddle, our Merchant of Record, back to your original payment method.
