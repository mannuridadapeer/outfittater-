Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Rate My Outfit ("we", "us", "the app"), available at slayrate.com, helps you get AI-generated feedback on your outfits. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account info: your email address (and name, if you sign in with Google), used to create and secure your account.
  • Photos you upload: outfit photos you choose to rate.
  • Ratings & history: the AI's score, breakdown and tips, plus a small thumbnail of your photo, saved to your account so you can see your history.
  • Usage data: basic counts such as how many ratings you've done (to manage free limits) and, if applicable, a referral/discount code you used.
  • Payment info: handled entirely by our payment provider — we never see or store your card details.

How we use your information

  • To generate your outfit ratings and save your history.
  • To run your account, streaks and free/Pro limits.
  • To process Pro subscriptions and apply referral codes.
  • To improve and maintain the app.

Third-party services we rely on

  • Google Firebase — sign-in and database (stores your account, ratings and history).
  • Google Gemini AI — your uploaded photo is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the rating. We do not use your photos to train AI models, and we do not sell them.
  • Paddle — our payment provider and Merchant of Record, which securely processes Pro subscriptions.

Data retention

We keep your account data and ratings until you delete them or ask us to delete your account. You can request deletion anytime (see Contact).

Your rights

You can ask us to access or delete your data by emailing us. We'll respond within a reasonable time.

Children

Rate My Outfit is not intended for children under 13. Please don't use it if you're under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; we'll change the "last updated" date above when we do.
